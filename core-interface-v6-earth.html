About Core Interface

Trusted Partner.
Proven Results.

Core Interface is a veteran-owned small business providing comprehensive IT services, technology solutions, and cleared professional services to federal agencies across DoD, civilian, and intelligence communities.

Our team brings deep technical expertise, proven delivery methodologies, and a genuine mission-first commitment to every engagement — whether rapid deployment or a multi-year enterprise program.

  • Federal-Only Focus
